# Privacy and Legal

## Privacy

melearner is local-first.

- No account
- No telemetry
- No analytics
- No remote sync
- No update check
- No hosted course catalog

Local data paths:

- Database: `$HOME/.local/share/melearner/melearner.db`
- Logs: `~/.melearner/`
- User course files: wherever you keep them
