melearner-ui.ttf is a Modified Version that combines Geist Regular with
Noto Sans JP 2.004-H2 Japanese outlines. The Japanese subset uses the
Unicode 17.0 Unihan Joyo and Jinmeiyo mappings.

The complete licenses and copyright notices are installed alongside this
notice:

- OFL-Geist.txt
- OFL-NotoSansJP.txt
- Unicode-Data-Files-LICENSE.txt

Source provenance and the reproducible generation recipe are documented in
native-app/src/fonts/README.md in the melearner source distribution.
